Aarstider

Explore with Ada as she travels through the seasons in her dream. Aarstider is not only a game but also visual experience about dreaming where you get to enjoy 'the little things'

Enjoy the slow paced gameplay, day / night and season cycles, the dynamic soundtrack that changes based on your location

The dream characters are waiting to chat with you

The game is also very suitable for children

Supported languages: English, Italiano, Esperanto
